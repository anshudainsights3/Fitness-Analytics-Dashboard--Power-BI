# Fitness-Analytics-Dashboard--Power-BI
**Project Title:** Gym & Fitness Performance Dashboard  

An interactive **Power BI Dashboard** designed to track and visualize key fitness business metrics — including client base, revenue, trainer count, membership types, and profit trends — providing valuable insights for gym owners and fitness managers to improve business decisions and member retention.

---

## **Project Description**
The **Fitness & Membership Analytics Dashboard** gives a complete overview of a gym’s operational and financial performance. It enables tracking of clients, revenue, expenses, memberships, and profitability in one interactive view.  

The dashboard is designed to help gym administrators and managers:  
- Monitor **financial growth** and **monthly performance**.  
- Track **membership distribution** (Platinum, Gold, Silver).  
- Identify **top-performing months** and **retention patterns**.  
- Analyze **client engagement** and **trainer performance**.  

---

## **Tech Stack**
The dashboard was developed using:  
1. **Power BI Desktop** – for creating visuals and interactive reporting.  
2. **Power Query** – for data extraction, transformation, and cleaning.  
3. **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – for KPI calculations (Profit, Revenue, Expenses).  
4. **Data Modeling** – relationships between Clients, Memberships, and Finances.  
5. **File Format:** `.pbix` for development and `.png` for preview.  

---

## **Data Source**
The dataset (sample fitness business data) contains:  
- Client information and membership type (Platinum, Gold, Silver).  
- Financial data: **Revenue, Expenses, Profit**.  
- Trainer statistics and member activity logs.  
- Monthly membership trends (Active vs Expired).  

---

## **Features / Highlights**
**Business Problem:**  
Gyms often struggle to manage client engagement, membership renewals, and financial tracking due to scattered data across departments.  

**Goal of the Dashboard:**  
To provide a unified visualization that simplifies financial tracking, membership monitoring, and performance analysis for decision-making.

---

## **Key Questions Answered**
- What is the **monthly trend** of revenue, expenses, and profit?  
- Which **membership plans** (Platinum, Gold, Silver) are performing best?  
- How many **clients** and **trainers** are active currently?  
- Which **months** have the highest new memberships?  
- How can profitability and member retention be improved?  

---

## **Key Visuals**
- **KPI Tiles:** Total Clients, Total Revenue, Total Expenses, Total Trainers.  
- **Column Chart:** Monthly Revenue, Expenses & Profit.  
- **Donut Charts:** Membership Distribution (Active & Expired).  
- **Table:** Client Membership details with Completed Days.  
- **Bar Chart:** Monthly New Memberships (Max vs Min).  

---

## **Business Impact & Insights**
- **Profitability:** $3M profit achieved with balanced expense management.  
- **Client Base:** 100 active clients with varied membership levels.  
- **Revenue Growth:** $4M total revenue, indicating strong monthly inflow.  
- **Trainer Efficiency:** 20 trainers effectively handling diverse clients.  
- **Membership Health:** Platinum has highest retention; Silver shows higher expiry risk.
